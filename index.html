Product Strategy Layer

VP of Product Strategy

  • Owns the "what" and "why" of the product
  • Oversees Director of Knowledge Management and Product Management groups
  • Defines customer journey and product-market fit
  • Connects directly with strategic clients
  • Defines product vision and strategic roadmap
  • Routes features to appropriate functional areas
  • Leads cross-functional feedback loops

Director of Product

  • Keeps team aligned and cohesive
  • Maintains product vision and strategy
  • Creates high-level requirements for Product Managers
  • Responsible for growth and progression of Product Managers and UI/UX team members
  • Ensures consistent product execution
  • Bridges strategy to pod implementation

  • Ensure designs meet end-user needs
  • Support pod teams with implementation
  • Maintain design-specific CLAUDE.md files for AI context

Director of Knowledge Management

Documentation & Training

  • Oversees all documentation and training initiatives
  • Manages documentation writers
  • Ensures help documentation quality and accuracy
  • Coordinates training video production
  • Maintains knowledge base and user guides

Product Manager

Product Strategy Team

  • Owns end-to-end customer journey for their product area
  • Connects directly with customers and market to understand needs
  • Creates product vision and PRFAQs for new initiatives
  • Conducts customer discovery sessions and feedback loops
  • Translates customer needs into product requirements
  • Defines acceptance criteria and success metrics
  • Manages product backlog prioritization based on customer value
  • Conducts product approval through system demos and story validation

Documentation Writer

Knowledge & Training Team

  • Creates and maintains release notes
  • Develops training documentation
  • Produces walkthrough videos
  • Designs in-app click through paths
  • Ensures documentation consistency and clarity
Product Innovation Layer

VP of Product Innovation

  • Owns AI agent tuning and optimization across all pods
  • Evaluates and implements cutting-edge AI tools and platforms
  • Develops training programs for prompt strategy excellence
  • Establishes metrics and KPIs for pod productivity
  • Creates best practices for AI orchestration workflows
  • Ensures pods leverage latest AI capabilities effectively
  • Tracks ROI and efficiency gains from AI implementation

Prompt Strategists

  • Report to VP of Product Innovation for AI best practices
  • Embedded in pods to drive rapid feature delivery
  • Orchestrate AI agents to implement user stories
  • Manage parallel AI sub-agents for complex features
  • Validate and refine AI-generated solutions
  • Deploy features directly to production
  • Bridge product vision with AI execution capabilities
The Three Pod Roles

Each pod consists of 3 specialists working together as a self-sufficient team to deliver features from vision to production through AI orchestration.

Product Manager

1 per pod (may serve multiple pods)

Core Responsibility:

Define the "what" and "why" - translating customer needs into clear product vision and actionable stories

Key Activities:

  • Customer discovery and feedback synthesis
  • Feature definition with PRFAQs and acceptance criteria
  • Story breakdown and prioritization
  • Maintains product context in CLAUDE.md
  • Final approval for production releases

Prompt Strategist

1 per pod

Core Responsibility:

Execute the "how" - orchestrating AI agents to rapidly implement stories into production-ready features

Key Activities:

  • Orchestrate AI agents to implement user stories
  • Manage parallel AI sub-agents for complex features
  • Validate and refine AI-generated solutions
  • Rapid prototyping and iteration
  • Deploy features to production

Senior Engineer

1 per pod (may serve multiple pods)

Core Responsibility:

Ensure the "quality" - maintaining technical excellence and code standards

Key Activities:

  • Define and maintain code quality standards
  • Technical architecture decisions
  • Code review and mentorship
  • Maintains technical context in CLAUDE.md
  • Co-approval for production releases
